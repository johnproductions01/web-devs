Our Solution


From planning to execution, we wanted to make sure every minute of this event was special.

  1. The Company Musical – We started the evening with a breathtaking musical that captured the history and ethos of Berenschot. This wasn't just entertainment; it was an experience that moved the employees to tears.
  2. Interactive Direction – Who said employees had to be just spectators? With a simple push of the red button they could direct the evening, from introducing high-tech performances by LedLedgends to revealing the magic of Niek Takens.
  3. Music and Dance – The Theater of Hart van Holland became the stage for an epic night of dancing. And for those who preferred singing to dancing, there was the unforgettable karaoke bar.
